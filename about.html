About:

Our mission:

Routes North is an independent travel guide covering Sweden, Denmark, Finland and Norway. Founded in 2014, its aim is simple: to help travellers get the most out of a trip to the region regardless of their budget. No messing about – just helpful, honest travel advice.
Unlike the vast majority of publications writing about Scandinavia, we do not take free stuff or discounts on travel services. Why? Because it means we’re free to say what we really think.
All of the hotels, restaurants and attractions reviewed on this website have been visited in person, anonymously. Routes North was started by the British journalist Steve Vickers, who has worked on more than 15 guidebooks across Europe and Asia and has spent the past eight years exploring Scandinavia. The site now attracts more than 130, 000 unique users a month. Want to say hi? Contact us via Twitter or send us an email at: hello [at] routesnorth.com

On this website, you will find:

Tested travel tips from years of experience.
Interviews with other travel experts.
Case studies and profiles of other travelers from various backgrounds, genders, colors, and nationalities.
A community of supportive travelers to help encourage you to keep going.
Detailed cost breakdowns to help you better budget for your trip.
Travel tips that can be applied to any destination in the world.
In-depth reporting that lets you know which sites really do help you save money!
